🔒 privacy policy

Last updated: March 2026 — This policy applies to the game "Run My Pet". We are committed to player transparency.

✅ offline by design · no personal data · ad-supported

1. offline gameplay & local storage

Run My Pet functions primarily offline. All game data, including your local high scores, animal unlocks, and map progress, is stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to this data.

2. no personal information

We do not collect or store personal identifiers such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise physical location.

3. advertising (monetag)

To keep the game free to play, we display third-party advertisements provided by Monetag. These ads may collect non-personal technical information (such as your IP address, device type, and advertising ID) to serve relevant ads and prevent fraud. No personal identity information is shared with advertisers.

4. children's privacy (COPPA)

This game is intended for all ages. We do not use behavioral tracking or collect personal information from any users, including children under the age of 13, in compliance with COPPA and GDPR regulations.

5. third-party providers

For more information on how our service providers handle technical data, you may view their policies here:

  • GDevelop Privacy Policy
  • Monetag Privacy Policy

6. data deletion

To delete all game data, simply uninstall the app or clear the app's cache in your device settings.
